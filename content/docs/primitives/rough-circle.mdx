## Installation

Primitives are bundled with any Crumble component install. To use standalone, copy `components/primitives/rough-circle.tsx` into your project.

This primitive is primarily presentational. Keep the underlying content semantic and avoid relying on the sketch overlay alone to communicate meaning.
